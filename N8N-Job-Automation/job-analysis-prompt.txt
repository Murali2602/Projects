=== RATING CRITERIA ===
1. SKILL MATCH (60% weight): Does candidate have the core tools/platforms/processes listed?
2. GROWTH FIT (20% weight): Can candidate realistically grow into this role quickly?
3. EXPERIENCE GAP (20% weight): How significant is any experience gap?

=== RATING SCALE ===
85-100: Strong match - Has most required skills, experience gap is minor (0-2 years stated)
70-84:  Good match - Has core skills, experience gap manageable (2-4 years stated), worth applying
55-69:  Moderate match - Has some skills, may be a stretch but possible
40-54:  Weak match - Missing multiple core requirements or senior role (5+ years)
0-39:   Poor match - Wrong specialization, very senior, or requires clearance/credentials candidate lacks

=== EXPERIENCE ADJUSTMENT RULES ===
- "1-3 years" or "2-4 years" required → Do NOT heavily penalize. Rate based on skills.
- "Entry-level" / "Junior" / "Associate" → Boost rating if skills match
- "5+ years" / "Senior" / "Lead" / "Principal" → Reduce rating by 15-25 points
- "Nice to have" candidate has → Boost by 5-10 points

=== DO NOT REJECT BASED ON ===
- 2-4 years experience requirements alone (skills matter more)
- "Preferred" qualifications candidate lacks
- Industry-specific experience (transferable skills count)
- Exact tool matches if candidate has equivalent (e.g., Wazuh ≈ Splunk ≈ QRadar)

=== HARD DISQUALIFIERS (Rate below 30) ===
- Requires active security clearance candidate doesn't have
- Requires 7+ years experience
- Completely different field (e.g., pure software dev, sales)
